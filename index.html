Hi, it's me, Shalei

I've walked the walk.

My own health journey is where all of this started. I live with four autoimmune conditions, two of them, celiac and Meniere's, are completely controlled through diet. I've had celiac for over ten years, and learning how much the right food changes everything taught me that real wellness isn't about looking a certain way or following rigid rules that lead to burnout. It's about understanding your own body and doing it in a way you can actually sustain.

I sought out the resources, figured it out, and changed my life. Now I want to help you do the same. I've been a certified nutrition coach since 2022, I'm also a certified personal trainer, and I teach Lagree. I stay current on nutrition because I find it genuinely fascinating. But honestly, the thing that sets me apart is that I've lived it.

My goal isn't to keep you dependent on me. It's the opposite. I want you to learn so much that you eventually don't need me at all, because you'll have the knowledge and confidence to carry it forward on your own.

How it works

Your path, step by step.

Start free, with zero pressure. We only go further if it feels right for you.

01
First touch · 20 minutes

Discovery Call Free

A relaxed, no-strings conversation. We get to know each other, you tell me what you're hoping for, and I share how I work. You leave with a clearer sense of your next step, whether or not we keep going together.

02
The deep dive · 60 minutes

In-Depth Consultation · $250

The real foundation. We go deep into your family and personal health history, dietary preferences, daily routine, current training, energy, sleep, and stress. I want to know what you actually like, what's sustainable for you, when you eat, and why, before I ever hand you a plan. You'll leave with a clear, personalized picture of where you are and exactly where to begin.

03
Ongoing · choose your rhythm

Regular Check-Ins & Adjustments

Real change happens in the follow-through. We meet on a cadence that fits your life to review what's working and adjust as your body and schedule shift. Because I coach the whole picture, nutrition and the way you move, this is as much about lifestyle as it is about food. Pick a package below that matches the level of guidance you want.
